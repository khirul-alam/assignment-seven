# KeenKeeper

**KeenKeeper** is a personal relationship management tool designed to help you nurture and maintain meaningful connections. In a fast-paced world, it’s easy to lose track of when you last spoke to a friend or mentor—KeenKeeper acts as your personal "shelf" for relationships, reminding you to tend to the bonds that matter most.

## Technologies Used

* **Framework:** Next.js
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
* **UI Components:** DaisyUI & Lucide React Icons
* **Charts & Analytics:** Recharts
* **Notifications:** React Hot Toast

## Key Features

### 1. **Smart Contact Tracking & Status**
Never wonder who to call next. Each friend is assigned a status—**Overdue**, **Almost Due**, or **On-Track**—based on your personalized relationship goals. The dashboard gives you a clear visual of who needs your attention today.

### 2. **Interactive Interaction Timeline**
Log your calls, texts, and video chats with a single click. Every interaction is saved to a beautiful, filtered timeline, allowing you to look back at your history of connections and see how much effort you've put into each bond.

### 3. **Visual Friendship Analytics**
Understand your social habits through data. The **Friendship Analytics** page uses dynamic charts to show the distribution of your communication types (Calls vs. Texts vs. Videos), giving you a bird's-eye view of your social life.
